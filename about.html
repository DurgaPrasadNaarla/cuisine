About Andhra Cuisine

At Tigers Truck, we bring you the rich and vibrant flavors of Andhra Pradesh, crafted with passion and tradition. Our dishes are made from fresh, locally sourced ingredients, ensuring an authentic taste in every bite.

Our Story

Founded by food enthusiasts who cherish Andhra's culinary heritage, Tigers Truck is dedicated to sharing the unique spices and recipes that define this region. We believe in quality, authenticity, and a warm dining experience.

Our Values

  • Authenticity: Staying true to traditional Andhra recipes.
  • Quality: Using fresh and natural ingredients.
  • Community: Supporting local farmers and producers.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Delivering delicious meals with excellent service.
